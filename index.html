Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

MENU ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

MENU does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data — including recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, and preferences — is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All app data is stored on your device using local storage. Your data is not synced to any cloud service or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is nothing for us to share.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at jbcommans@gmail.com.
